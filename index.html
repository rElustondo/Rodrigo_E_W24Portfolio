Capstone Project

  • Project Summary: To develop a web application designed to facilitate the connection between homeowners and contractors, enabling efficient hiring for a range of outdoor property maintenance services, including but not limited to lawn care, gardening, snow shoveling, and driveway maintenance.
  • Project Vision: to connect customers to gardeners,lawn mowers, snow shovelers and driveway sealers to customers seeking outdoor property services in an efficient and user friendly manner. In this document, we will outline the purpose, scope, and key definitions relevant to our services.
  • Project/Business Requirements:
    • Be able to connect contractors with home or property owner
    • Where contractors or homeowner can review past jobs
    • Contractors and homeowner to confirm their upcoming jobs
    • Homeowner should be able to view the contractors rating and past work before hiring
  • Project Plan:
    • Login system
    • A web app that can be accessed from any browser from a computer, mobile device, or any other device that supports browsing
    • A booking and messaging system connecting customers to contractors
    • A template for creating and maintaining contractor profiles
    • Review and rating system for clients to rate contractors
    • Online calendar for contractors and clients to confirm their upcoming jobs
    • Page to review past jobs completed with a gallery of images
    • Cost and budget calculator
    • Integrating a payment API to enable online transactions
    • FAQ page
    • Dispute resolution system
  • Wireframes/Mock ups: Application-Mock-Up
  • Status Reports: Status Report 2 summary: The Outdoor Property Maintenance Web App is making steady progress in providing users with tools to manage their outdoor spaces efficiently. Completed features include profile image saving, availability sections, and access to contracts. The project aims to enhance user experience and functionality, enabling clients to schedule services, access support, and showcase their spaces. Future steps involve finalizing features, testing for quality assurance, and preparing for deployment. The project's focus on usability and reliability promises to deliver a robust and intuitive solution for outdoor property maintenance needs.
